Name: Lucy Heartiflia

Age: 17

Magic: Celestial Spirit Magic

History: Lucy was born in the once extremely wealthy and powerful Heartfilia family.Her father and mother were originally part of a merchants' guild called "Love & Lucky" where they met; they decided to become independent when Lucy's mother, Layla, became pregnant. They chose her name when they noticed that the sign of the guild was missing the letter "K," and instead read 'Love & Lucy'; it made an impression on them, so they decided to name their daughter after it.
